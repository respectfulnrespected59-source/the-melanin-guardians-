# Scene 04 — I Will Never Forget

## Narrative Beat
Continuous from Scene 03's final frame. The corridor holds its new brighter glow. The farthest ancestor is dark. The ripple has been received. Now — for the first time — we see his face. The cold open closes with his name and his first vow. The ancestors named him as he named himself. Same breath. Same sound. Then one line — carried in a voice the series will hear again and again — and the world holds.

**0:04–0:06 — THE NAME (simultaneous vocal)**

The chorus is older, layered, distant. His voice is close, single, young, but carries the same timbre — as if he is one more voice in the line finishing a word the line has been holding for ten thousand years.
