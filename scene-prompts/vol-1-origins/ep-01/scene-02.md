# Scene 02 — The Corridor of Lineage

## Narrative Beat
Continuous from Scene 01's final frame. His amber eyes are open — and the world dissolves. The savanna doesn't cut away; it peels back like a veil, revealing that he has always been standing inside something older. He is now in the Lineage Corridor: an infinite procession of ancestors stretching behind him into deep time. He is the front of the line. The last born. The one who woke.

## Visual Prompt (Kling 3.0)

**Subject:**
Same young Black man, now standing — bare-chested, dark jeans, amber glow still faint in his veins. Eyes wide, not afraid, absorbing. He occupies the foreground, back three-quarters to camera, facing the corridor ahead of him. His silhouette is the anchor.

**Setting:**
The Lineage Corridor: an impossible space that reads as both interior and exterior. The floor is smooth dark stone, cracked like dried earth — same texture as the savanna. The walls and ceiling don't exist — only a deep void filled with slow-drifting particles of gold light. Stretching behind him in a straight line: ancestor figures, each slightly more distant, each slightly more stylized — the nearest nearly photorealistic, the farthest purely geometric silhouette. Hundreds deep. All Black. All standing. All still, facing the same direction he faces.

**Lighting:**
The corridor has no external light source. Each ancestor figure emits a faint subdermal glow — the same melanin-activation motif from Scene 01, carried across all figures. The nearest ancestors glow warmest (ember gold). Those in the deep distance glow cooler (pale amber, almost white-gold). Guardian Prime's glow is brightest. He is the culmination.

**Camera Language:**
Begin at the exact frame Scene 01 ended — medium-close on his face, amber eyes just open. At 0:01, slow pull-back begins: the savanna grass at the edges of frame dissolves upward like evaporating mist revealing the void. By 0:04 we are at medium-wide, the first ancestor figures visible behind him. Pull-back continues to wide by 0:08 — the full corridor visible, receding to infinity. Camera holds at 0:08. Steady throughout, no shake.

**Motion:**
The savanna peels away — grass and dust rising and dissolving upward at the frame edges, not wiping or cutting. The ancestor figures are motionless. Only the gold particles drift slowly downward like slow ash. At 0:07, the nearest ancestor — directly behind him, 10 feet back — turns their head slightly toward him. Subtle. A few degrees only. Almost unnoticeable on first watch.

**Atmosphere:**
Silence made visible. The void breathes. The particle drift is meditative. This is sacred space — not threatening, not cold. Warm despite the darkness. The weight of ten thousand years of survival is present but not heavy — it is offered, not imposed.

**Color Palette:**
Onyx void, cracked-earth dark stone, ember gold (subdermal glows), deep umber (skin tones throughout), pale gold (distant ancestors), soft amber (particle drift). No blues in this scene — that was the savanna's color. This space is all warm dark.
