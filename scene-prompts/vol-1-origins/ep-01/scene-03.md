# Scene 03 — The Ignition

## Narrative Beat

Light ripples outward from him in a slow concentric pulse and reaches the nearest ancestor — the one who turned his head at the end of Scene 02. The ancestor's own subdermal tracery answers: amber lines bloom across his chest, his throat, his temples, matching Melvinci's signature. The corridor itself warms a full register in response. This is not a power. It is a recognition. A credential being accepted.
