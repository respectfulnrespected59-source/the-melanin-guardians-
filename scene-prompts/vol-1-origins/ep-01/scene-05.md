# Scene 05 — Title Card

## Narrative Beat

Hold on Melvinci's face from the end of Scene 04 — eyes still amber-lit from within, corridor reflection still alive in them. The camera begins a slow, inexorable push toward his right eye. The iris fills the frame. The push continues past the surface of the eye into the reflected corridor itself, as if the audience is passing through him into the space he just left. The corridor recedes into depth. In that depth, amber-gold light gathers — first as scattered points, then as lines, then as letters. The words THE MELANIN GUARDIANS form from the light itself. As the title settles, the light solidifies: the letters gain weight, texture, the surface of carved warm stone catching amber light from below. Beneath it, a second line resolves the same way but quieter: VOLUME I — ORIGINS. Hold. The title breathes once with the ancestor rhythm, then the frame fades to black over the final beat.
