# Osanyin — Character Sheet

Quick production reference. For full lore and cultural notes, see `characters/bibles/osanyin.md`.

---

## Identity

- Name: Osanyin (Yoruba-rooted family legacy name)
- Codename: The Nearest
- Relationship: Paternal grandfather of Melvinci; previous Guardian Prime
- Age (Corridor appearance): mid-60s, vital
- Status: Passed from waking world before Scene 01

---

## Appearance

- Build: Lean, upright, narrower in the shoulders than Melvinci
- Skin: Deep umber, warm-toned (match Melvinci's family palette)
- Head: Short gray hair, close-cropped. Short well-kept gray beard.
- Eyes: Dark brown. In Corridor: amber-lit from within, slightly cooler than Melvinci's for generational read.
- Wardrobe (Corridor): Dark simple wrap, waist to mid-calf. Bare-chested. Barefoot.
- From Episode 2 onward: add a thin cord necklace of small dark wooden beads.
- Subdermal glow: Amber-gold tracery. Brightness = Melvinci's Scene 01 baseline (so Melvinci's Scene 03 ignition reads as exceeding his grandfather's).

---

## Voice

- Pitch: Lower baritone than Melvinci, drier, slower
- Delivery: Unhurried, complete, no wasted breath
- Casting direction: Gravity of John Kani, stillness of Delroy Lindo (quiet register), warmth of Danny Glover (grounded)
- First line (Episode 2): *"I know your name. Do you know mine?"*
- Language: English primarily. Yoruba words where no English equivalent exists (àṣẹ, orí, ìran), translated in context.

---

## Powers / Abilities (Corridor State)

- Native lineage memory — he is part of the Corridor
- Light-recognition and corridor-signal modulation (match, amplify, dampen)
- Naming (Guardian Prime ability — to name in the Corridor fixes identity in the lineage record)
- Primary teaching function for Melvinci across Vol. 1
- Cannot leave Corridor, cannot touch waking world, cannot override Melvinci

---

## Arc (Vol. 1)

- Ep 1: Silent. Head-turn (Scene 02), ignition (Scene 03), threshold presence
- Ep 2: First speech. Name reveal. First teaching.
- Ep 3: Affected by antagonist strike on Corridor — first dim
- Ep 4-6: Primary teacher. Teaches Melvinci at a cost we define later. Volume ends with understanding that Melvinci's entries cost Osanyin.
