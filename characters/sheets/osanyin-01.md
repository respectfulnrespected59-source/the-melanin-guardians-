# Osanyin — Character Sheet

Quick production reference. For full lore and cultural notes, see `characters/bibles/osanyin.md`.

---

## Identity

- Name: Osanyin (Yoruba-rooted family legacy name)
- Codename: The Nearest
- Relationship: Paternal grandfather of Mello; previous Guardian Prime
- Age at death: mid-60s, vital
- Time of death: when Mello was 13
- Time in the Corridor at Scene 01: ~12-15 years
- Status: Passed long before Scene 01 — NOT recently deceased

---

## What He Taught Mello (Before Death)

- How to build
- How to fix a bike
- How to box (to stand, not to fight)
- Never called it "the practice" — believed the calling would arrive on its own

---

## Appearance

- Build: Lean, upright, narrower in the shoulders than Mello. Boxer's build in youth.
- Skin: Deep umber, warm-toned (match Mello's family palette)
- Head: Short gray hair, close-cropped. Short well-kept gray beard.
- Eyes: Dark brown. In Corridor: amber-lit from within, slightly cooler than Mello's for generational read.
- Wardrobe (Corridor): Dark simple wrap, waist to mid-calf. Bare-chested. Barefoot.
- From Episode 2 onward: add a thin cord necklace of small dark wooden beads (worn smooth).
- Subdermal glow: Amber-gold tracery. Brightness = Mello's Scene 01 baseline.

---

## Voice

- Pitch: Lower baritone than Mello, drier, slower
- Delivery: Unhurried, complete, no wasted breath — concentrated by 12 years of silence
- Casting direction: Gravity of John Kani, stillness of Delroy Lindo (quiet register), warmth of Danny Glover (grounded). A man who has waited.
- First line (Episode 2): *"I know your name. Do you know mine?"*
- Language: English primarily. Yoruba words where no English equivalent exists (àṣẹ, orí, ìran), translated in context.

---

## Powers / Abilities (Corridor State)

- Native lineage memory — he is part of the Corridor
- Light-recognition and corridor-signal modulation (match, amplify, dampen)
- Naming (Guardian Prime ability — to name in the Corridor fixes identity in the lineage record)
- Patience (canonical — a capacity for stillness across 12+ years)
- Primary teaching function for Mello across Vol. 1
- Cannot leave Corridor, cannot touch waking world, cannot override Mello. Could not reach Mello's trial.

---

## Arc (Vol. 1)

- Ep 1: Silent. Head-turn (Scene 02 — carries 12 years of waiting), ignition (Scene 03), threshold presence
- Ep 2: First speech. Name reveal. First teaching. (Mello's body is on his prison bunk during this.)
- Ep 3: Affected by antagonist strike on Corridor — first dim
- Ep 4-6: Primary teacher. Teaches Mello at a cost we define later.

## Key Family Context

- Mello inherits directly across the gap.
