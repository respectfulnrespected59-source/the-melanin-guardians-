# Melvinci — Guardian Prime

The first of the line to wake in this generation. The one the corridor recognized.

---

## Identity

- **Name:** Melvinci (pronounced *mel-VIN-chee*)
- **Codename:** Guardian Prime
- **Age:** mid-20s at series start
- **Origin:** To be revealed in Episode 2. For now: he wakes in the savanna with no explicit backstory on-screen. The lack of context is intentional — the cold open establishes who he becomes before it tells us who he was.

---

## Appearance

- **Build:** Lean, strong, athletic but not bodybuilder-huge. Dancer-warrior proportions.
- **Skin:** Deep umber, warm-toned. Never rendered washed-out. Specular highlights always warm gold, never white.
- **Head:** Shaved. No facial hair in Vol. 1 — he will grow a short beard by mid-Vol. 2.
- **Eyes:** Default dark brown. Amber-lit from within when melanin-activated. When in lineage-memory state, the ancestor corridor is reflected inside them (see Scene 04 for the lock).
- **Wardrobe (Vol. 1, Ep. 1):** Dark jeans, bare-chested. No shoes in the corridor sequence. This will evolve — regalia enters in Episode 3.
- **Subdermal glow:** Amber-gold tendrils tracing the veins, brightest at wrists, throat, sternum, temples. Intensity varies with state. See Scene 01 for the baseline look.

---

## Voice

- **Pitch:** Low baritone, unhurried.
- **Delivery:** Flat and true. He does not emote at his listener — he speaks as if each line is the act of deciding something.
- **Signature line:** *"I will never forget."* First spoken in Scene 04, recurring.
- **First voice direction for casting:** Think the gravity of a young Chadwick Boseman, the cadence of a young Mahershala Ali, the stillness of a young Lakeith Stanfield. Not an imitation of any of them — that composite.

---

## Powers / Abilities

- **Melanin activation:** Subdermal light response to lineage states. First triggered in Scene 01.
- **Lineage memory access:** Can enter and witness the Corridor of Lineage. Scene 02 is his first access. Each visit carries a cost we will define across Vol. 1.
- **Light-ripple recognition:** As demonstrated in Scene 03 — his presence triggers response in the ancestor line. This is not a weapon. It is a credential.
- **What he cannot yet do:** Combat powers, flight, shielding. All that comes later. Vol. 1 is about awakening, not fighting.

---

## Arc (Vol. 1)

- **Episode 1 — Origins:** Cold open awakening. Corridor. Ignition. Name reveal. First vow.
- **Episode 2:** The nearest ancestor (head-turn from Scene 02) is named and speaks to him. Melvinci's real-world origin begins to surface.
- **Episode 3:** First antagonist enters. Not a fight yet — a theft. Something is taken from the Corridor.
- **Episode 4–6:** TBD. Arc of Vol. 1 ends with Melvinci stepping back out of the Corridor into the waking world, changed.
